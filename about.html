About

Isabel Okanese (she/her) is an Award winning artist, Spiritual entrepreneur and mentee at the Indigenous Friends Association’s IndigiTech program. After having completing the IndigiTech program, Isabel is dedicated to furthering her understanding and experience of coding, UX design, and digital art. Isabel has just launched a new branch to her Okanese Transformative company called Indigii CreeTure Design where she highlights both her Digital and Traditional art based on storytelling and spiritual knowledge from her Oji-Cree and Dene Cultures. Isabel's goal is to create an Isabel-Metaverse where clients all over the world can access her spiritual services through virtual reality. Clients will be able to interact with her avatar or book a live interaction and join her in various places from her life such as her childhood family trap-line, or sit in her Tipi or do ceremony in Edmonton River Valley or the ocean in Victoria BC. Isabel plans on furthering her digitial education by taking Interaction Design Prototyping for XR at UBC Extended Learning centre, Digital Marketing Bootcamp with Alacrity Academy, I-Cedar Entrepreneur Indigenous Course via UVic business program. Isabel also plans on networking to build a team to collaborate on building the Isabel Metaverse. Isabel is a powerful force in her community, personal and work life and uses her can-do attitude and tireless commitment to encourage others to work hard and succeed. Isabel is inspired daily by her Ancestors. In her free time, Isabel likes to sit by the ocean, learn her Nêhiyawêwin and talk to the dead a.k.a. ghosts.
